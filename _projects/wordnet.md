## Overview
Project which explores how the volume of printed works in English has changed over time, as well as the structure of the is-a relationships of words

## Description
- Integrates WordNet, a semantic lexicon for English, into NGordnet.
- Implements Hyponyms feature for retrieving specific synsets.
- Requires designing graph structures for efficient data handling.
- Emphasizes independent design decisions.
- Detailed specifications in the [FAQ](https://example.com/faq).
